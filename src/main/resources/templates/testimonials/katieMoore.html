Where to begin...my husband and I have been together since 2002 (we met at Mizzou). So, you know, young and able to eat anything with no side effects! Well time went on, children were born, and metabolisms slooooowed down. Neither of us were happy with how we looked and felt. We tried many different weight loss ideas (weight watchers, workout videos, and food tracking apps). We were not good at being accountable, and sticking with a program is hard when life is busy and junk food is easy and tempting.

....in steps Maggie. I had gotten her name from some friends who had great success with her. So we thought, why not? We called and within a few weeks we met and began the best journey ever. She taught us so much about meal planning and helped us figure out what works best for our individual nutritional needs. Maggie is amazing at keeping you motivated, positive, and focused on your goals. She is always available to help, even in tricky situations (like parties, how to eat smart at restaurants, and dealing with the aftermath of the occasional bad day). She never makes you feel “bad” for messing up a meal, and helps you refocus on what you want to accomplish! I have recommended her to many others looking for help, and will continue to do so for a long time!
